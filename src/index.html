Help Section

      Frequently asked questions
      add_a_photo
    • How do I add a photo?

      • To add a photo simply press on the upload button, which will open a new window where you can select a given picture you'd like to upload.

      cached
    • How do I swap photo?

      • To swap photos, simply select the picture you'd like to swap, and hold in your cursor on a given picture and drag it over the picture you'd like to swap with.

      insert_photo
    • How do I preview a photo?

      • Click on the photo you'd like to preview. To close the preview click on the background or click the cross in the top right corner.

      edit
    • How do I edit photo?

      • To edit a given photo, simply upload a photo and a new pop-up will appear, where there's different kind of buttons to edit your picture with. If happy with the result click "upload", otherwise click "cancel" to cancel.

      color_lens
    • How do I change theme?

      • To change theme find the "Themes" button in the navigation bar, whereafter you have 4 themes to choose from.
